07

Brand Voice

FPG copy must always feel direct, human and confident. We talk to people, not at them. We say what we mean. We never hide hard truths behind jargon.

Direct
No jargon, no waffle. Say it plainly.
Human
Real language, real warmth, real care.
Confident
We know our subject. We do not hedge.
Responsible
Hard truths delivered with care.
Write this

"If you die tomorrow, your family could lose the house. We can make sure they don't."

Not this

"Our flexible protection solutions offer comprehensive peace-of-mind coverage."

Write this

"You've built something worth protecting. Let's talk about how we keep it safe."

Not this

"FPG provides market-leading financial planning services for discerning clients."

FCA reminder: All financial promotions must be fair, clear and not misleading. All public-facing copy must be reviewed against COBS/MCOB requirements and signed off by Compliance before issue.
