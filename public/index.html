Video Social Content

Video is the single most powerful content format available to you right now. Across every major platform, video consistently outperforms static images -on LinkedIn, video gets 5× more engagement than any other content type. On Facebook, video posts generate 59% more engagement than static posts, and on Instagram, Reels receive 22% more interaction than standard image posts. The numbers are not close.

For platforms like Instagram and TikTok, there is no question -user generated content (UGC) is where the real engagement happens. Content that feels authentic, filmed on a phone, in the moment, consistently outperforms polished branded video when it comes to reach and interaction. If you are comfortable in front of a camera, this is where the biggest opportunity lies.

That said, you can still achieve strong results with non-UGC content. Branded video -used consistently and shared at the right times -builds recognition, reinforces trust, and keeps FPG visible in feeds. The assets in this library are ready to use across your social channels right now. Have questions or want to talk through a content strategy? Book a session with Mark -mark.ninnim@financeplanning.co.uk or 07525 932 299

Static Social Content

This library contains pre-approved social content ready to post across your channels. Before creating anything yourself, browse here first -if what you need already exists, it has been signed off and is good to go.

If you do choose to create your own content, it must be submitted for approval before being posted. Our Principles

Trusted. Local. Advice.
Trust is earned, not assumed, and once earned it must be kept. We are rooted in the communities we serve, building long-term relationships based on honesty and genuine care. Our service is advice-led, not sales-driven. Local knowledge, local faces, and local accountability matter. Our clients know who to call, and we answer.
Technology that serves people
We use technology to make the human experience better, not to replace it. Tools support our advisers and free up more time for the conversations that matter. They enhance our service, but they never substitute it.
Treat people as we'd wish to be treated
Every client deserves the same standard of care we would want for our own family. We listen, we explain clearly, and we make sure no one ever leaves a conversation feeling confused or undervalued. Good advice starts with being heard.
Emotional Intelligence
The opposite of artificial intelligence, and what keeps us grounded as people helping people. We never forget that everyone is different. Behind every application is a life and a family unique to that person. We strive to listen properly, respond with empathy, and deliver the very best in customer service.

Tone in Practice

Write this

"If you die tomorrow, your family could lose the house. We can make sure they don't."

"You've taken out a £280,000 mortgage. Here's what happens to it if you can't work."

"We'll give you a straight answer -and if something isn't right for you, we'll say so."

Not this

"Our flexible protection solutions offer comprehensive peace-of-mind coverage."

"We leverage cutting-edge technology to deliver a seamless client journey."

"As a leading provider, we're committed to empowering your financial wellness."
